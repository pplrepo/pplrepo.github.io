Keeping Up With Society

August 9, 2023

Going nowhere fast...

Part of the problem with society today is everything is moving entirely too fast. You wonder why you are always stressed, feeling ill, irritated and stuck in "go" mode. We are literally trying to keep up...but keep up with what exactly? Growing older quicker? That's exactly what all that stress is going to lead (aging in fast forward motion).

There's no amount of skin cream or magical product that will rid you of that reality. Stop looking for a quick fix. I hate to be the one to tell you, but it doesn't exist. Quick fixes are what I like to call a capitalism hoax. Which is basically companies making money off our insecurities.

Slow down...take time to heal forreal; from the inside out.

Do more research on the hacks, crystals, and gimmicks you are seeing online. (Tip: Real research is unbiased and goes beyond reading one or two articles. Good research habits also involve reading from multiple points of view.)

Be mindful of the company you keep around you and how you respond to people and situations. (Are you setting clear boundaries?)

Self reflection and awareness is essential in your healing as well. What part do you play in both the growth and dysfunction aspects of your life.

Embrace the moment. Take in the process. Look at what you've come through and where God allowed you to be.

Don't take the ability and opportunity to heal for granted.

Healing is and will forever be a process. It's not a sprint. Rushed healing usually does not turn out well or effective.

Word of advice: use social media as a tool and stop looking at it as "goals". Learn a thing or two then turn it off.

Live in the moment and allow yourself to "be here now".

Allow your body and mind to heal at its natural pace.

Draw closer to God. Block out the noise, turn off the distractions. (Please believe... the world will still be worlding, you are not missing anything more important than your health and definitely don't need to try to "keep up". )

You're so busy comparing your pace to others and society that you can't appreciate where you are, what you have, and what you have the ability to do.

Healing takes time, so take your time.

Always consult with a medical professional before trying anything you found online. This information is not intended to be medical advice.

Wishing you peace and good health.

3 John 1:2
