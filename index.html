What is a Doula?

A doula is a trained and experienced professional who provides continuous physical, emotional, and informational support to a birthing person (and their partner if they have one) before, during, and after birth.

Doula Services by Joy

My Mission: to help women adapt to their transformations while providing comfort and support through physical and emotional healing

I support families throughout the pregnancy, birth, and postpartum stages. Each stage of the journey to meeting your little one is filled with many emotions and tons of information. This can be a lot to register on top of day to day life and symptoms connected to your overall experience.

Studies have shown higher rates of having a positive birth experience when a doula is present.

With the support of a doula there is: decreased anxiety, improved postpartum mental health, better breastfeeding outcomes, improved relaxation, and boosted self esteem.

I am here to help relieve any stress you may have and provide you with as much knowledge and support as needed.
