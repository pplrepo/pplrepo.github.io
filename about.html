About Me

I am a trained Doula and owner of Postpartum Lifestyle. I support families during the fourth trimester as it can be one of the most challenging periods in the journey of becoming a parent. The postpartum period too often has the least amount of support available for some families.

I have personally experienced the effects of the after birth period and grew a great passion towards the subject. In lieu of this, I birthed the Postpartum Lifestyle to provide my experience based knowledge, resources and support to other women and families, in hopes to help their transition be as smooth as possible.
