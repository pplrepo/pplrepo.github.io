About Me

Welcome to Postpartum Lifestyle! Thank you for stopping by and checking out what I can offer during your journey.

My name is Joy, I am a trained Doula and the owner of Postpartum Lifestyle. I graduated from the University of North Carolina Greensboro with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration and minor in American Sign Language. I previously worked as a Nurse Aide, Pharmacy Tech, and Wellness Ambassador for a combination of 5 years. I possess a broad understanding of medical terminology and have 4 years of knowledge with health insurance terminology, claims, and benefits.

I support families throughout the pregnancy, birth, and postpartum stages. Each stage of the journey to meeting your little one is filled with many emotions and tons of information. This can be a lot to register on top of day to day life and symptoms connected to your overall experience.

I have personally experienced each stage and especially felt the effects of postpartum and grew a great passion towards the subject. In lieu of this, I birthed the Postpartum Lifestyle to provide my experience based knowledge, resources, and support to other women and families. My goal is to help you adapt to your transformation and support you through your healing.

There is an abundance of information available for moms during the full experience of bringing a little one into the world. Overall, I am here to help relieve a vast portion of any stress you may have and to provide you with as much knowledge and support as needed. I want to ensure you feel empowered about your choices during this special time.

Please feel free to contact me directly to learn more about my services.
