Get Your Mind Right

August 15, 2023

You can think yourself out of anything.

How does this apply to where you are in life and what you want to accomplish next?

Whether it is getting a new position at your job, starting a new business, breastfeeding for 1 year, having an unmedicated birth. \

However this applies to your situation, understand your next move starts in your mind. If you don't think you can do it, you won’t.

If I had a nickel for everytime I heard someone say they couldn’t do something. I could hire someone else to write these blogs for me. No, but really…

No matter how you spin it, if you don't think you have the ability to do something, you will stop (or never start) doing what it takes to accomplish said thing.

Sometimes we think like this because there are internal battles we have not addressed.

Your next move starts from within. Here is a list of things you can do to help kick start your journey to getting your mind right:

- Do some soul searching.

- Pray for guidance and clarity.

- Take control of your health.

- Make sure you are getting adequate rest.

- Have you been taking your vitamins?

- Be still and listen to your thoughts.

- Write out your goals, fears, and current accomplishments.

- Write out your support system.

- Clean up your social media (remove anything/anyone that you find discouraging)

- Significantly limit your time on social media and replace that time with quieting your mind and learning new skills.

Once you get your mind situated, you will be surprised at the things you can do.

Above all, keep your faith. I believe in you, and I am rooting for you.

Wishing you peace and good health.

3 John 1:2
